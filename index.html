ABOUT The #INMYOWNSKIN movement

Welcome! What are we about?

We have designed this website as a safe space for anyone who wants to express their thoughts and feelings about the way the way they look. This is a space for body acceptance. No one is perfect, and no one is the same. Despite what you may hear or see in social media today, everyone has something they are unhappy with or wish they could change. And you know what, that’s totally okay and normal.

This website is a space for people to share the things they may be unhappy with in how they look. Whether it may be your body shape or size, skin colour, cellulite, hair, literally anything. What you may find here is that you are not the only one, and you are certainly not alone in not being always happy with the way you look all.

What we believe is that everyone, even the people we may perceive as having a “perfect look,” have something they wish they could change. Would it not be better to accept that fact, and join together in it, and acknowledge that we are all struggling in some way or another.

What we are hoping to achieve and create here is, a space where it is okay to be insecure about certain things about ourselves, because in all honesty, who isn’t? We hope that our website will create a safe space for anyone who wishes to share their story, or day to day experiences and thoughts. With other people who may relate to those experience and thoughts.
